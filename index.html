Data Artisans uses data obtained from Google Trends and the National Statistics Institute (INE) to create artistic 3D visualizations. The platform allows users to explore and analyze data interactively, facilitating the identification of trends and relationships that are not evident in static 2D representations. This approach appeals to the recipient's emotions while enriching the user experience by transforming data analysis into a more intuitive and visually appealing activity..
