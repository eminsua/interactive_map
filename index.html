Data Artisans dives into the future of spatial statistics and interactive 3D data visualization. Our open-source project is changing the paradigm of data visualization for decision-making, especially in the urban context.

Data Artisans: Transforming Data Analysis and Urban Management Through Open Source

At Data Artisans, our goal is to investigate the impact of advanced spatial statistics and interactive 3D data visualization on urban decision-making. We believe in the power of open source and sharing real-time data to transform data analysis and urban management.

Navigate and Orbit in a World of Data with Data Artisans

Data Artisans presents 3D infographics that allow you to navigate and orbit among them. These visual representations, available in real-time and open source, allow you to explore data in a completely new way.

Collaborate with Data Artisans: An Open-Source Project for City Planning and Management

At Data Artisans, we not only present practical applications of these technologies, but also foster collaboration. Our open-source project invites everyone to contribute and help shape the future of our cities.
